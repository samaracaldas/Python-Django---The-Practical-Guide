Taylor Swift releases her
tenth studio album

By Samara
Last updated on November 10th

Taylor Swift released her tenth studio album this Friday (21st). "Midnights" has thirteen tracks, written and produced alongside Jack Antonoff and also a participation of Lana Del Rey in one of the songs.

Taylor, 32, describes this new work "as a collage of intensity, ups and downs and ebbs". On her Instagram profile, she says it's a concept album in which the thirteen songs form "a complete picture of the intensities of that crazy and mysterious hour".

The first track is "Lavender Haze," a term Swift said she first heard while watching the "Mad Men" television series about being in love, as she described the album's first single "Anti-Hero" as one of her favorite songs she's ever written, in which she delve into her insecurities. The clip for this track was the first to debut.
